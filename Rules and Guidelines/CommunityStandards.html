Community Standards

1 - Core Behavior Standards

  • - Minimum age to access any platform: 13 years old.
  • - Avoid harassment, hate speech, slurs, and political, discriminatory, or religious discussions.
  • - Trolling, flamebaiting, spam, or disrupting the experience of others is prohibited.
  • - Do not impersonate members, share misinformation, or falsely report others.
  • - Keep the platform safe and avoid unwanted communication.

2 - Platform Use and Content

  • - All communication must be in English (UK/US).
  • - Content must be safe for work for members above 13; NSFW, extremist, suicidal, violent, or gory content is prohibited.
  • - Personal information, private messages, or recordings cannot be shared without consent.
  • - Self-advertisement, referral links, or monetization links are only allowed in designated channels.
  • - Follow channel-specific rules; illegal content is strictly forbidden.

3 - Community Operations & Enforcement

  • - Follow moderation staff guidance.
  • - Report rule violations promptly.
  • - Do not discuss moderation actions publicly.
  • - Using alternate accounts or external programs to bypass punishments is prohibited.

4 - Intellectual Property

  • - Sharing copyrighted material is allowed only if the user owns the rights.
  • - Do not share pirated software, media, or content.
  • - Respect trademarks, patents, and other intellectual property rights.
  • - Give proper credit to original creators when sharing content and follow their licensing terms.
